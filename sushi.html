Palm Sushi

Using local and seasonal ingredients is the most important element in Japanese cuisine. It also has become the primary principle for the international culinary community. With commitment to that philosophy, Palm Sushi features seasonal ingredients of the Pacific Northwest in authentic Japanese preparation. Due to COVID-19 Pandemic, our sales revenue has decreased 54%. Our current issue in the restaurant is that due to the governor’s current guideline, we are only allowed to have less than 50% of indoor-dining; however, for most sushi restaurants, our customers prefer dining in. Therefore, we are losing a significant amount of our customers.

Here’s what you can do to help us through this difficult time:

1. Ordering takeouts from us. This is the safest dining option for both you and us.

2. Ordering from UberEats, Doordash, or our official website. We are offering free delivery within three miles around the restaurant.

3. If you are passionate about marketing, free feel to contact us and help us with creating more marketing ideas for our business.

Thank you for taking your time to help us going through this tough time, and we wish you stay healthy and stay safe.
