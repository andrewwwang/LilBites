Pho Shizzle

We first opened Pho Shizzle in January of 2020 with excitement and hope. Soon after we opened our doors, we were forced close almost immediately due to extremely low order requests. We believe many of our customers are hesitant to take Pho to go, but we are confident that our new take out method will offer an experience nothing short of eating at our tables.

Pho Shizzle recently reopened for business and we are also offering dine in for parties under 8 people.
