GUSTEAU'S

★★★★☆

Gusteau’s is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving the Greenlake area for the past 40 years. We pride ourselves in our consistency to our loyal customers. After the COVID pandemic, we have seen a 67% decrease in revenue. Although this has been somewhat mitigated by efforts to utilize delivery apps such as GrubHub or UberEATS, business still remains slow. Unfortunately, we fear that we may need to close our door indefinitely in the near future.

In order to help our business’ door stay open we ask our customers to do the following: leave us a good rating on yelp, UberEats, etc, post a photo on social media with #Gusteas, refrain from dining at our restaurant in parties of five or more, keep your masks on the whole time except for when you are eating.
