About Us

WHAT IS LIL BITES?

Lil Bites was founded through DubHack20 as a comprehensive, user-friendly local struggling small restaurants directory website that makes local small restaurants the focal point. We knew that as we grew and expanded as a company, it would become increasingly difficult to maintain a directory that consisted of multiple industries. Food made sense to us, because everyone needs to eat, and it has cultural significance. We are the people who we are because of our communities, Gathering and food play a key role in defining our sense of community, and Lil Bites provides users with a tool that gives them an opportunity to locate these communities ad support our small local restaurants.

Mission Statement

To be the no.1 supporter of our local businesses and communities.
