Mama's Kitchen

Bellevue, WA, is home to one of the region’s best Korean food hotspots. Mama’s Kitchen has all of your favorite authentic Korean Asian food options with our own special twist on them. We don’t follow the typical Asian restaurant experience. Every item on our menu has Mama’s signature sauce, Mama’s marinade, or Mama’s unique way of prepping the food. We push Korean comfort food to another level. The dining experience here is like eating in your own Mama’s kitchen—clean and comfortable, giving you space to enjoy food that’s prepared with love and care. That’s why Mama’s Kitchen is Bellevue’s favorite Korean food destination. However, since the COVID-19 started, we have been loosing more 85% of our sales revenue. It has been a huge struggle for us to pay our employees on time, and we had to lay off five of your employees.

Here are three things you can do to help us:

1. Come to the restaurant and dine with us! We are currently open with limited capacity. We are dedicating ourselves to take precautions and making your dining experience as safe as possible.

2. We also offer delivery service on major apps. Order food from Doordash and Uber!

3. Tag us in your instagram or Facebook post.
